About ImanQuiz

Strengthening faith through knowledge

ImanQuiz is an interactive web application designed to help Muslims deepen their understanding of Islam through engaging quizzes and educational content. Our mission is to make Islamic learning accessible, enjoyable, and meaningful for everyone.

Whether you're looking to test your knowledge of the Quran, Prophets, or Pillars of Islam, ImanQuiz provides a platform for continuous learning and spiritual growth.
